Football Manager scouting tools

These tools are designed to help you find talented young players in Football Manager games. They were created using data from FM23, but may be relevant for other versions of the game.

Scouting map

A world map showing the average youth production of each country and club in the game. Can be filtered by database setup to see the effect of adding players & leagues.

Personality checker

A tool to visualise the possible hidden attributes for a player, based on their personality & media handling style.

Frequently asked questions

Further info on the game setup and results of testing.
